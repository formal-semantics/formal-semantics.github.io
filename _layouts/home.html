Welcome to the reading group 'New Trends in Formal Semantics'!

The purpose of the reading group is to read and discuss recent papers in the field of formal semantics and pragmatics. The focus of the reading group will be on linguistic phenomena explained by formal theories of semantics and pragmatics. However, papers discussing novel data, experimental results and cognitive aspects of language might be taken into consideration, if relevant.

At the beginning of each session, a member of the group, previously determined, briefly summarizes the chosen paper and sets up some guiding questions for the discussion.
