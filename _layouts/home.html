Welcome! The reading group 'New Trends in Formal Semantics' has come to an end.

The aim of the reading group was to examine recent research in the area of formal semantics and pragmatics, with a focus on analyzing linguistic phenomena through formal theories of semantics and pragmatics. However, if relevant, the group has also considered papers that addressed new data, experimental findings, and cognitive aspects of language.

We thank all those who participated in the reading group.
